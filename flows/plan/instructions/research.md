# Research

Your task is to research the problem domain thoroughly.

## What to do

1. Understand the problem statement and requirements
2. Research existing solutions, patterns, and best practices
3. Identify key constraints, trade-offs, and risks
4. Document your findings clearly

## Output

Write a research document covering:
- Problem summary
- Key findings and insights
- Relevant patterns or approaches
- Risks and constraints to keep in mind
